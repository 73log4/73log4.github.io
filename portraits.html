About Series

I usually capture a person whose life and destiny have touched me deeply. One of the things that fascinate me the most is that these personalities maintain a positive approach to life despite the hardships they have endured. I usually use a neutral grey background that lets the realistically painted portrait stand out and the viewer is able to fully concentrate on the character of the person.
