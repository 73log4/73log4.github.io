About Series

This cycle of paintings includes landscapes inspired by real places in Israel Nature parks - among others Nahal Amud Nature Reserve, Hermon Stream Nature Reserve, Dor Habonim Beach Nature Reserve, Yehudia Forest Nature Reserve. It was presented together with my environmental photographs in En Afek Nature Park gallery in spring 2022.

The nature in Israel is beautiful and unique. It is a kind of beauty which is not always visible at first glance. Very often it is necessary to spend hours hiking through arid landscape, so that in front of you as a miracle appears a valley full of greenery, birdsong and the roar of water. But also the generous beauty of the coast always enriches me. Endless sand dunes interwoven with low silver green vegetation make them stand out constantly changing spectacular cloud scenery in motion.
