Main Collections

Portraits

I usually capture a person whose life and destiny have touched me deeply. One of the things that fascinate me the most is that these personalities maintain a positive approach to life despite the hardships they have endured.

Landscape

This cycle of paintings includes landscapes inspired by real places in Israel Nature parks - among others Nahal Amud Nature Reserve, Hermon Stream Nature Reserve, Dor Habonim Beach Nature Reserve, Yehudia Forest Nature Reserve.

View in Gallery ❯

Existential

In this cycle the themes of harsh conditions and germinating life are intertwined as a symbol of the fact that life can miraculously develop and survive even in totally inhospitable conditions.
